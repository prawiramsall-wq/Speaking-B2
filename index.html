Beginner Speaking Exercise 2

🖼️ Picture Description

Look at the picture and speak for 30–60 seconds. Use the guiding questions to help you.

Exercise 1 of 8

• What can you see?
• Who is in the picture?
• What are they doing?

Exercise 2 of 8

• Where is this place?
• What activities can people do here?
• Would you like to visit it?

Exercise 3 of 8

• What objects can you see?
• What is happening?
• Describe the scene.

Exercise 4 of 8

• How many people are there?
• What are they doing?
• How do they feel?

Exercise 5 of 8

• What place is shown?
• What do you like about it?
• Have you ever visited a similar place?

Exercise 6 of 8

• What is the weather like?
• What are people doing?
• What would you do there?

Exercise 7 of 8

• Describe the people.
• What activities are happening?
• What do you think about this picture?

Exercise 8 of 8

• What is the main thing in the picture?
• Describe it.
• Why is it interesting?
